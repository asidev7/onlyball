How the drawing works & your odds

Every 100 $BALL you hold earns one ticket in that night's drawing, with no cap. Your odds of winning any given drawing are exactly (your tickets) / (total tickets sold that night) — visible on your account page before every drawing. OnlyBall makes no guarantee that you will win, and past results are not indicative of future outcomes.

The winner is selected by a provably-fair algorithm, not by OnlyBall staff. See /fair to verify any past drawing, or download the verification script.

Responsible gaming

From your account page you can:

  • Set a personal weekly deposit cap.
  • Self-exclude for 7, 30, or 90 days — this immediately signs you out and blocks deposits, purchases, and withdrawals for the selected period.

If you feel your gambling is a problem, resources such as the National Council on Problem Gambling (1-800-522-4700, US) offer free, confidential support.

What $BALL is (and isn't)

$BALL is an internal accounting balance tracked in OnlyBall's database. It is not a token deployed on any blockchain, is not transferable outside your OnlyBall account, and has no value outside of earning tickets for the nightly drawing. USDT deposits and withdrawals are processed via NowPayments in USDT-TRC20 (Tron network). The nightly drawing separately uses a public Solana blockhash purely as an unpredictable random beacon (see /fair) — no funds ever move on Solana.
